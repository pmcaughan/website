Service

ENDEAVR

This semester, I'll be working with the ENDEAVR organization. ENDEAVR stands for Envisioning the Neo-traditional Development by Embracing the Autonomous Vehicles Realm. It is a project lead by A&M to take students and use technology to revitalize small towns. As part of this project, I am working with civil engineering, urban planning, architecture, and other computer science students to help use technology in order to make the city of Nolanville, TX more walkable and less car dependent.

Hurricane Harvey

In my high school senior year, Hurricane Harvey came in and devastated large parts of Houston, my hometown. In the aftermath, I worked with other members of my school robotics team to help clean up rubble and damaged drywall from a flooded general practitioner's office.
